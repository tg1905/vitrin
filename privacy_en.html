Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Thank you for choosing the Vitrin: Seller Price Analyst browser extension ("Extension", "We"). Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data our extension collects or does not collect, and how it stores it, in full compliance with Google Chrome Web Store policies.

1. What Data We Collect (Do Not Collect)

Our extension operates with a strict "Zero Data Collection" principle:

  • Personal Information: We do not access, collect, or transmit any of your name, email address, credit card, or Trendyol account information (passwords, cookies, shopping carts) to our servers.
  • Browsing History: We do not track which websites you visit. The extension is solely focused on reading public price information on specific (trendyol.com) product pages that you designate.
  • Server Communication: Our extension does not have its own database or cloud server. The extension only sends anonymous requests during the license verification phase and to pull public product details.

2. How Data is Stored

All data you enter into the extension (e.g., links to the products you want to track, your target prices, your check frequency preferences, and your PRO license key) is saved securely and locally within your own browser using the chrome.storage.local API.

This means your data physically never leaves your computer. If you uninstall the extension, all this data is completely deleted from your computer.

3. Third-Party Services (Lemon Squeezy)

To provide advanced tracking features (PRO version), we offer license sales via Lemon Squeezy. The purchasing process is completely securely completed on Lemon Squeezy's own infrastructure. The extension only checks whether the license key you obtained from Lemon Squeezy is valid via their required API. During this process, no financial or personal information is transmitted to us.

4. Permissions Used by the Extension

  • storage: Required to save your tracked products locally.
  • alarms: Required to securely run scripts in the background to check prices at the intervals you set.
  • notifications: Required to send you desktop notifications when a price drop occurs.
  • host_permissions ("*://*.trendyol.com/*"): Required to read public price values on Trendyol product pages.

5. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When critical updates are made, you will be notified in the relevant sections within the extension.
